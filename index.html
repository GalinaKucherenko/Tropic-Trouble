How to Play

  • 1

    Swap and match three or more similar items to clear them from the board and earn points

  • 2

    Each level has specific objectives, such as reaching target score or collecting certain items

  • 3

    Use power-ups strategically to clear larger sections of the board and help complete levels

  • 4

    Engage with the storyline as you progress through levels and uncover new surprises on your tropical island

  • 5

    Complete daily challenges to earn bonuses and rewards that help you on your journey

Features

  • Immerse yourself in a colorful tropical world filled with exciting puzzles and adorable characters.

  • Experience a variety of levels with unique challenges that keep gameplay fresh and engaging.
